Any Questions?......

We Have The Answer To Regularly Asked Stuff

Can I Use You To Move Telephone Wiring And Sockets.

Yes. as long as the wiring and sockets are within the boundary of your property, we are good to go.

If the fault  or work required is in the "Network" - outside your home - maybe overhead or on a telephone pole, then the company you pay your 'phone bill has to fix it, free of charge in most cases.

My Provider Says The Fault Is Internal. Is This Correct?

When you have a line fault, this causes slow broadband, noisy telephone calls, no dial tone and other frustrating problems.

Your provider should test the line. The test sends signals from your local telephone exchange to try and determine the distance from the exchange to the fault and what type of fault it is.

The test can on occasion cause the line fault to briefly repair itself. This sends a false reading to the provider who will tell you either no fault found or it's internal.

If this happens, don't book a visit with their engineer, they can charge up to £180 even if they don't find a fault.

Call us now. We will carry out a home visit and diagnose the problem accurately for less than half price.

If the fault is in the network, you can claim back our fees from your provider.
